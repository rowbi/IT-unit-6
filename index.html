About Us

Hot Beans Website development is a web dev company based in the south east of england. We started in 2010 and have been operating ever since. We specialise in custom web development, e-commerce, Search Engine Optimisation, current website analysis and hosting. We have worked with many big names on their websites from small sections to full sites which focus on user engagement. Futhermore, our team have collaborated alongside top universities and helped pave the way for the future of web development. With over 25 years experience we are confident that our step by step process will help any business or individual boost their online prescence as its something that we enjoy and is engrained into our values. Hot Beans was originally a coffee shop which ran IT clinics, then we had numerous people enquire on how to make websites so we transformed the coffee shop into a web studio where we we recieved an overflow of people. We then saw an opportunity to expand so we bought our new head office in Reigate where we employ over 30 members of staff all with the same passion for web design as the Directors. We really hope that we can work alongside to boost your digital online prescence.

What we do

The process

After you have submitted on our contact form, you will then recieve an initial welcome email from our support team which will gauge a rough idea of what your digital needs are, then we will connect you to one of our specialists in that area who will book you in for a 45minute timeslot to onboard you and get as much information as possible. After you are happy with the plan that our team makes you will make a 20% deposit then we will work alongside you each step of the way, we will setup a local server on our site, for example "{business_name}.hotbeanswebdevelopment.co.uk" and you will be able to check on the work we are doing once you recieve your login, after the website is done you will pay the full amount (or payment plan) then we will work out a hosting cost + optional maintenance cost (usually £25-£50pm) which we will aquire the domain name of your chosing then host your website and confirm final details.
